# User Personas — Learnify NUST

Learnify NUST is designed around the real needs of the NUST academic community. The following user personas represent the primary and secondary users of the platform.

---

## 1. Undergraduate Student (Primary User)

### Profile
- NUST undergraduate (1st–8th semester)
- Enrolled in technical or non-technical degree programs
- Frequently uses online resources for academic learning

### Goals
- Understand course concepts clearly
- Access exam-relevant material quickly
- Learn at their own pace outside lectures

### Pain Points
- Difficulty finding structured content for specific topics
- Confusion due to excessive, unfiltered online resources
- Lack of guidance on what to study and in what order

### How Learnify NUST Helps
- Section-wise playlists mapped to course topics
- Trusted, curated academic resources
- Simple and intuitive navigation

---

## 2. Senior Student / Content Contributor

### Profile
- Final-year undergraduate or graduate student
- Strong understanding of specific subjects
- Willing to support junior students

### Goals
- Share knowledge and help juniors
- Build academic and professional credibility
- Preserve learning materials for future batches

### Pain Points
- No formal platform to share structured academic content
- Contributions often lost in chats or cloud drives

### How Learnify NUST Helps
- Ability to upload or link curated playlists
- Recognition as a content contributor
- Long-term academic impact of shared knowledge

---

## 3. Faculty / Teaching Assistant (Future User)

### Profile
- Instructor or Teaching Assistant at NUST
- Responsible for academic guidance and instruction

### Goals
- Provide supplementary learning resources
- Ensure students follow a structured learning path

### Pain Points
- Limited visibility into external resources used by students
- No centralized platform to recommend academic content

### How Learnify NUST Helps
- Ability to recommend or approve learning resources
- Better alignment between classroom teaching and online content

---

## 4. Platform Administrator

### Profile
- Technical team or project owner
- Responsible for system maintenance and content moderation

### Goals
- Ensure platform security and reliability
- Maintain academic content quality

### Pain Points
- Risk of low-quality or irrelevant submissions
- Managing access control and moderation at scale

### How Learnify NUST Helps
- Admin dashboard for approvals and moderation
- University-email-based authentication system

---

## Summary

These personas ensure Learnify NUST remains:
- Student-centric  
- Academically focused  
- Scalable for future growth
